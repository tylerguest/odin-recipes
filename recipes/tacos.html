Tacos

Description

The absolute BEST ground beef Tacos! An easy to make homemade taco seasoning and a secret ingredient that makes this the most flavorful and juiciest taco meat EVER! The perfect family weeknight meal that can be ready in less than 30 minutes!

These classic Ground Beef Tacos feature homemade taco meat loaded into freshly fried taco shells. Serve up with all your favorite toppings for an easy family meal that takes less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • Cumin
  • Chili Powder
  • Oil
  • Butter
  • Corn Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook the ground beef, cook it up until itâ€™s completely browned. Drain the grease. Then when we return to the heat to the stove lower the heat, add the homemade taco seasoning and a small amount of tomato sauce.
  2. Allow the taco meat to simmer briefly in the sauce to give more flavor.
  3. Fry the corn tortillas in a small amount of oil in a medium size skillet. Once fried on both sides, remove the tortillas and fold in half to form their shape.
  4. Fill the taco shells with the cooked beef and any toppings desired.
